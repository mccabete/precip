- The daily climatology is generated from IMERG V07B Final Run. It was accessed on https://gpm.nasa.gov/data/imerg/precipitation-climatology 03/24/2026. The climatology is from 2001 to 2022. 


- The Anomolies were calculated by subtracting the daily climatology from "GPM IMERG Final Precipitation L3 1 day 0.1 degree x 0.1 degree V07 (GPM_3IMERGDF)". Accessed from here https://disc.gsfc.nasa.gov/datasets/GPM_3IMERGDF_07/summary?keywords=%22IMERG%20final%22
